ABOUT THE GAME

Simple Rules.
Endless Satisfaction.

Sliding Puzzle is a classical puzzle game enjoyed by players of all ages.
The objective is clear: move the pieces into the correct positions to assemble the image.
You can only slide tiles into that one empty spot, so you've got to think a few moves ahead - trust me, it's trickier than it looks.
The final goal is not only to complete the picture but also to position the empty square in the bottom right corner.
Looks simple enough, right? Yeah, that's what I thought too. Then I spent 20 minutes on a 4x4 grid.

HOW TO PLAY

After a while, you'll start seeing patterns and getting faster at figuring out the right sequence of moves.

Choose your difficulty level (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5)

Tap or slide a tile next to the empty square

Continue rearranging until the image is complete

Make sure the empty square ends in the bottom right corner
